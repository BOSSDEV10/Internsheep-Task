The Story

Our Classic Cappuccino is a tribute to the timeless Italian café tradition. We pull a precise double shot from our signature BrewHaven espresso blend — a mix of Colombian Supremo and Ethiopian Yirgacheffe — then top it with perfectly steamed microfoam milk and a dusting of Valrhona cocoa. Every cup is crafted to order by our skilled baristas.

Espresso Steamed Milk Cocoa Foam Valrhona Cocoa
