☕ Menu & Food

Absolutely! We have a wide range of vegan-friendly options across our entire menu. Any drink can be made vegan by substituting dairy milk with oat, almond, or soy milk at no extra charge. Our avocado toast can be made 100% vegan, and we have dedicated vegan dessert options. Look for the 🌱 symbol on our menu board.

Yes! Our kitchen offers several gluten-free items including our cheesecake (GF crust on request), certain salads, and most of our beverage range. Please inform our staff about any allergies or dietary restrictions when ordering, and we'll take every precaution. Note that we're not a certified gluten-free kitchen, so cross-contamination may occur.

Of course — customisation is our love language! You can choose your milk type (dairy, oat, almond, soy, coconut), adjust sweetness levels, choose between single/double/triple espresso shots, add flavour syrups (vanilla, caramel, hazelnut, lavender), and specify extra hot, iced, or blended. Just ask your barista!

Our house espresso blend is a rotating mix of Colombian Supremo and Ethiopian Yirgacheffe, crafted and roasted in-house every week. We also offer single-origin pour-overs that change monthly, sourced from farms across India (Coorg, Chikmagalur), Ethiopia, Kenya, Brazil, and Colombia. Every monthly origin is described on our menu board.

🏠 Visiting Us

We're open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and on weekends (Saturday & Sunday) from 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM. On public holidays we operate from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Kitchen service closes 30 minutes before closing time.

Yes! We offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi (500 Mbps fibre) for all our guests. Connect to the "BrewHaven_Guest" network — password available at the counter. We also have dedicated power outlets at most tables and a quiet work zone upstairs, ideal for remote workers and students.

We have a small private parking lot behind the café accommodating up to 12 cars (first-come, first-served). Street parking is also available on Brew Lane and the adjacent 3rd Cross Road. We're also very well connected to public transport — the nearest bus stop is a 2-minute walk away.

Yes! We accept all major payment methods including Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, American Express, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), and cash. We don't accept cheques. Our minimum card payment is ₹50.

🎵 Events & Bookings

Yes! We love hosting private events. Our space can accommodate up to 50 guests for private hire (buyout) or up to 20 guests for a semi-private event in our courtyard. We offer tailored packages for birthdays, corporate events, product launches, photo shoots, and more. Email us at events@brewhaven.in to get a quote.

You can register for any of our events through the Events page on our website, by calling us at +91 80887 76655, or by emailing events@brewhaven.in. We recommend booking early as our workshops sell out fast — typically within 48 hours of announcement. Payment secures your spot.

⭐ Loyalty & Offers

Collect BrewPoints with every purchase — earn 1 point for every ₹10 spent. Redeem 100 points for any drink up to ₹350 value. Points never expire as long as you visit at least once every 6 months. Members also get early access to new menu launches, priority event registration, and surprise monthly gifts.

While we believe the BrewHaven experience is best enjoyed in-café, we do offer limited delivery via Zomato and Swiggy for select items. Please note that due to transit time, beverages are not delivered — delivery is available for bakery and food items only. For the full menu experience, visit us in person!

❓ General

We love families! BrewHaven is fully child-friendly with a dedicated kids' menu and high chairs available on request. Our courtyard is also pet-friendly — dogs are welcome in the outdoor seating area. We even have a little water bowl station for your furry companions!

Yes! Our lush green courtyard at the back of the café offers outdoor seating for up to 16 guests. It's shaded by a canopy of plants and fairy lights — particularly beautiful in the evening. The courtyard is also where most of our live events take place.

Yes! Our in-house roasted beans are available for purchase in 250g and 500g bags at the counter (₹380–₹680 depending on origin). We also sell BrewHaven branded merchandise including ceramic mugs, travel cups, and tote bags. Perfect gifts for the coffee lover in your life!
