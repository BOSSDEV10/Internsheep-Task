 Recipe Title: Veg. Pasta

 Author: Doe

 Published Date: December 10, 2025



 Description:

     Vegetable Pasta is a Simple, Healthy, and Flavorful dish made with fresh vegetables, herbs, and pasta. It is perfect for a quick lunch or a comforting dinner and can be prepared in under 30 minutes.


 Ingredients:

  1. 200 grams pasta
  2. 1 cup chopped bell peppers
  3. 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  4. 1 medium onion (chopped)
  5. 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  6. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Black pepper to taste
  9. 1 teaspoon mixed herbs

 Equipment Needed:

  1. Large cooking pot
  2. Frying pan
  3. Strainer
  4. Wooden spoon
  5. Knife and cutting board

 Method:

    ৹ Boil water in a large pot and cook pasta according to package instructions.

    ৹ Drain the pasta and keep it aside.

    ৹ Heat olive oil in a frying pan.

    ৹ Add chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until golden.

    ৹ Add bell peppers and mushrooms, and cook for 5 minutes.

    ৹ Add salt, black pepper, and mixed herbs.

    ৹ Add cooked pasta to the pan and mix well.

    ৹ Cook for another 2 minutes and turn off the heat.


 Quick Tips:

    Tips
    • Use fresh vegetables for better flavor.
    • Do not overcook the pasta.
    • Add a splash of pasta water for extra moisture.

 Variations:

    ৹ You can add grated cheese, olives, or chili flakes for extra taste.

    ৹ Chicken or shrimp can be added for a non-vegetarian version.


 Nutritional Information (Approximate):

    ৹ Calories: 350 kcal

    ৹ Protein: 10 g

    ৹ Carbohydrates: 45 g

    ৹ Fat: 12 g


 Notes:

     This recipe is best served fresh. Leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for up to one day.
